How much detail should the class diagram include?

More detail = richer reference, but harder to read at a glance

A

High-level (Recommended)

Show all classes and their relationships (inheritance, composition, associations) — but only the most important fields and methods on each. Fits on one readable page.

B

Full detail

Every class with all public fields and methods listed. Complete reference, but dense — likely needs a large or landscape page to remain legible.
